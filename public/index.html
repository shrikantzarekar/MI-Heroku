Advantages of Condition Monitoring @ Jalgaon Plant

"Our people are the heart and mind of our organisation, our machines are its strong arms."

We produce highest quality cement, and to make it happen, health of our machines is utmost importance. This systems helps our team to monitor and take proactive decisions w.r.t plant health.

Real time condition Monitoring

Through this system, our team can monitor important parameters related to plant condition.

  • No doubt CCR was always a phone call away- but now it is as easy as just a tap on your mobile screen
  • Trends and graphs for complete visibility on machine health when ever and where ever you are.
  • More than 100 parameters being monitored and still more will be added

Relavant SMS Alerts to our teams

  1. Automatic SMS alerts will be send to concerned people, with relavent information.
  2. Information on Location, criticality, current value and type of alert is included in the SMS.
